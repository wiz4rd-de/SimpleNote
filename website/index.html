QuickScrawl

A tiny notepad that stays out of your way.

Rich text on ruled paper. Cross-platform. ~10 MB.

App screenshot goes here

Features

✏️

Rich text formatting

Bold, underline, strikethrough, and colors — all the basics, nothing bloated.

📝

Ruled notepad styling

Yellow paper with ruled lines and a handwritten font. Just like the real thing.

💾

Auto-save

Your notes are saved as you type. No Ctrl+S anxiety.

🔍

Font zoom

Ctrl/Cmd +/- to zoom in and out. Easy on the eyes.

📌

Pin to foreground

Keep your notes always on top while you work in other apps.

🌙

Dark mode toolbar

A dark toolbar that adapts to your system theme preferences.

🪶

Tiny footprint

~10 MB installed. No Electron. No bloat. Just Tauri.

💻

Cross-platform

Available on macOS, Windows, and Linux.
